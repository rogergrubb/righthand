## One-line positioning

**Every founder gets a right-hand.**
The personal AI you live with — voice in, voice out, sees your whole world, writes its own lessons every night, and compounds forever.

## Why we win

For 2,000 years, only emperors, generals, and CEOs had a trusted officer at their side. Someone who saw everything, forgot nothing, executed without being asked. That asymmetry is now obsolete. Every ambitious person can have one.

The moat is not the model. The moat is **the lessons DB + the daily trend report + the graph of your own world**, all written by and for you, every single day. Month 6 beats Month 1 not because the AI is smarter — because it knows *you* better.
